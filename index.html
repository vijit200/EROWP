ABOUT OILSOIL

We are on a mission to use eco- friendly solutions. We focus on providing the best car care by saving the environment and conserving water.
We plant trees in order to contribute towards the environmental causes, trees benefit soil, absorb the carbon dioxide, filter the water and clean the air.
We are equipped to wash the cars with less water usage and from properly disposing of waste to inspecting vehicles for excess emissions, our goal is to provide our customers with an eco-friendly alternative to vehicle servicing, repairs, and maintenance.
It is necessary to balance our earth while driving forward.

Recycling of Waste

We envision a world that is more connected to the environment. We don’t want to leave carbon footprint on our environment; we want to leave it cleaner for the next generation. We adhere to strict recycling measure.

Preventing Spillage of Hazardous Oils/Fluids

Spillage happens at every garage, but our purpose-built drains direct the spillage to sewer, via an oil separator. These drains are checked and cleaned regularly to remove toxins and debris.
